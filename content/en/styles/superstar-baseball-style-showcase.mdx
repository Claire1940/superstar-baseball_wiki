## Mastering the Frozen Swing Mechanics

The "Frozen Swing" is the signature move of the Blizzard style. In this **superstar baseball style showcase**, we’ve observed that the swing physics are slightly different from the base game. When the Frozen Swing is triggered, the ball's trajectory seems to stabilize, reducing the "drop" or "curve" applied by the pitcher's abilities.

### Timing Adjustments for Blizzard
One of the most jarring aspects of switching to Blizzard is the timing shift. Most players are accustomed to the "Perfect" timing window found in Mythic styles. Blizzard, however, rewards a slightly different rhythm:

*   **Early Swings:** Often result in line drives that clear the infield with ease.
*   **Late Swings:** These are surprisingly effective, often turning into high-arcing home runs toward the opposite field.
*   **Perfect Swings:** Result in a "Frozen" effect on the ball, making it nearly impossible for outfielders to track efficiently.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Switching between styles frequently can ruin your muscle memory. If you commit to the Blizzard style, spend at least 30 minutes in the practice cage to adjust to the new timing.
