## Pitching Mechanics and Strategy

Pitching in this game is a game of "dots." Unlike other Roblox baseball titles where you might drag a cursor, here you must precisely click where you want the ball to cross the plate. Mastering the various pitch types is essential for any aspiring ace.

### Standard Pitch Types

1.  **Fastball:** The bread and butter of any pitcher. It relies on pure velocity to blow past the batter.
2.  **Sinker:** A deceptive pitch that appears to stay high before dropping sharply at the last second.
3.  **Slider:** Moves horizontally across the zone, perfect for forcing foul balls or swinging strikes.
4.  **Curveball:** A slow, looping pitch designed to disrupt the batter's timing.

## Batting and Base Running Essentials

Hitting in 2026 requires a mix of patience and pattern recognition. The game utilizes a "Power vs. Contact" toggle system. 

*   **Power Mode:** Increases the chance of a home run but shrinks your contact window. Best used when the pitcher is spamming predictable fastballs.
*   **Contact Mode:** Increases the size of your hitting reticle. This is essential for putting the ball in play against "glitchy" pitches like the Shadow or Sinker.

### Advanced Base Running

Base running is more than just clicking the next base. You must utilize the "Rocket Boost" and "Slide" mechanics to avoid being tagged out. In a 7v7 environment, the defense can pass the ball with incredible speed, so every millisecond counts.
